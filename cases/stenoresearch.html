Thumbnails & Coverarts

The possibly best way to understand a product fully, is by getting a visual explanation.
This was the purpose for my coverarts and thumbnails at Steno Research: Give the users a summary of what this article, podcast and even video is about.

The production-process of these thumbnails and coverarts simply started with a few keywords. My bosses and collegues gave me a list with keywords about the specific content, including some wants, needs and ideas for the final outcome. My job was to create and bring these ideas to life, with the purpose to catch and get the users/subscribers attention.

You can view my coverarts attatched to the different articles, and my thumbnails as SoMe posts to promote podcast-episodes.



Milliardærklubben

​"Milliardærklubben" is a Danish podcast that offers independent analyses of financial markets, geopolitics, and economic trends.
Hosted by economist Andreas Steno Larsen and geopolitical strategist Mikkel Rosenvold, the podcast aims to provide listeners with insights that are free from capital or special interests.
The podcast covers a wide range of topics, including market trends, political developments, and economic forecasts, with episodes featuring discussions on subjects like the impact of geopolitical events on the global economy and analyses of specific industries.
"Milliardærklubben," which also hosted live events such as a session at Bremen Teater on February 8, 2023, announced its closure in November 2023 after being "cancelled by Berlingske," marking the end of its run under that name.

My influence and responsibilities with "Milliardærklubben", was moslty the marketing and administration of the SoMe channels.
This involved tasks such as: Creating video-teasers, frame the quotes and best parts of the episodes, thumbnails and teasers for the new aired episodes, promote the liveshows and even arrange competitions for the fanclub.
You can view all my work on the Milliardærklubben Instagram-account



Asgard-Steno Global Macro

Asgard-Steno Global Macro is a hedge fund launched on December 1, 2024, through a collaboration between economist Andreas Steno Larsen and Asgard Asset Management. The fund combines human decision-making with AI-driven models to identify and capitalize on opportunities across various asset classes, including commodities, equities, fixed income, and foreign exchange.

A lot was happening during the launching-process of the fund, especially with the marketing and brand identity. For months I had feedback-sessions and disucssions with the board about what to involve in the design and how to present the fund as professionel as possible. Overall, I was the creator, designer and the one responsible of the logo, colorpalette, website-design and developing. I even developed a form-system, with the purpose to make it easier and automate the process, for the clients, of applying and signing up in the fund.

Check out the final outcome on https://www.stenoglobalmacro.com/.
