Mellow Archives



Mellow Archives is a Copenhagen-based slow-fashion brand that reinterprets classic menswear and womenswear with a modern, minimalist aesthetic.
Their designs often feature high-quality materials like 15oz selvedge denim and Spanish merino wool, blending raw and refined elements to create timeless pieces.
The brand emphasizes exclusivity, releasing limited-edition collections that are not restocked once sold out. This approach fosters a sense of rarity and uniqueness among their offerings.
Mellow Archives maintains an active presence on social media platforms such as Instagram and TikTok, where they showcase their latest drops and engage with their community.

Webdesign & Webshop

The overall design of this webshop is rooted in a minimalistic and trendy aesthetic, carefully balanced with a sense of timelessness. Utilizing a grid-based structure, the layout ensures clarity and visual harmony, guiding the user effortlessly through the experience. A soft pink brand color is used strategically for calls-to-action, adding a subtle vibrancy that contrasts the otherwise calm and neutral palette. The homepage is structured to highlight the uniqueness of each clothing collection, dividing them with curated cover art followed by a filtered product grid that enhances usability. The brand's core identity – limited storage and archival, old-fashioned finds – is visually translated through its design, echoing a story of timeless fashion preserved and reimagined.

Although the webshop is built on a Shopify template, my role involved extensive customization with CSS to create a more distinct and brand-aligned interface. These custom touches helped elevate the site beyond a standard e-commerce layout, making it feel tailored and intentionally designed. Looking forward, the goal is to evolve the platform into a hybrid between a digital lookbook and a community gallery – showcasing real-life styling from customers and community members. This dual approach, blending inspiration with direct product access, will, among other things, be promoted through a campaign titled 'Join The Community', seamlessly connecting visual storytelling with the shopping experience.



Promotion & SoMe

The brand’s social media presence is driven by a fluid and intuitive strategy – without a rigid content plan, the focus lies in keeping the audience engaged and visually connected to the brand’s identity. Across platforms like Instagram, Pinterest, and TikTok, the goal is to build a consistent, recognizable vibe that mirrors the aesthetic and values of the brand. This is achieved through mood-driven visuals, carefully curated posts, and storytelling that captures the essence of the collections. Campaigns are created for special occasions (like Valentine’s Day), along with giveaways, teaser content for upcoming drops, and real-time updates on stock levels (e.g. “Only 3 left…”), all designed to foster excitement and urgency.

My role involves both creative direction and hands-on production of content. I design story elements and posts—working closely with the partners – while taking full ownership of more advanced content pieces. Simpler posts may include text overlays on influencer imagery, while more conceptual assets, like a vinyl player teaser for S3, reflect deeper creative execution. Beyond design, I actively contribute to brainstorming sessions: planning weekly content strategies, discussing campaign ideas, and crafting concepts to help keep the audience in the loop and emotionally connected to the brand.
